Center For Entrepreneurship
Student Business Development Worksheet

Note: Answer all of the questions that are in blue before you begin the official experience of starting your business. Keep notes during the experience. At the end of the experience, answer all of the questions in black. This serves as a final report.

Problem

  1. State the Problem that you are solving with your business:
    2. How has your understanding of this problem changed during your experience?

Company Overview

  1. What does your business do?
  2. How has this changed during your experience?
  3. Draw out a diagram of your Business Model.
  4. How has your business model changed during your experience?
  5. What is the value proposition of your business?
  6. What intellectual property does your business have?
  7. Describe any intellectual property that you have for your business. If you do not have any, describe any intellectual property that you think would benefit your business.
  8. List your strategic assets. (Facilities, equipment, supplies, etc)
  9. List your strategic assets.
  10. List your partnerships and how they help your business.
  11. What new partnerships do you have because of your experience? How do they help your business?
  12. Your facilities

Market Analysis, Marketing, Sales Strategy

  1. Describe your target customers. Why are these people likely to be your customers?
  2. Describe the groups of people who actually became your customers. Why did they purchase from you?
  3. What is the size of your market? (What is this based on?)
  4. How has your definition of the size of your market changed? Why?
  5. What methods will you use to create awareness of your product?
  6. How did you actually create awareness of your product? Which methods worked best? Why? Did any methods not work? Why?
  7. Who are your competitors? Why? (You must list at least 2 competitors).
  8. How are you different from your competitors?
  9. Who is your business actually competing against? Why?
  10. How are you different from your actual competitors?

Team

  1. Who is involved in your business? Provide a brief description of what experience they bring to the company and what aspects of the company they are involved with.
  2. How has the company team changed? If new people added, explain why, their skills, and what they do. If people have left, explain why and how you now complete the work they were originally doing.
  3. Do you have an advisory board? If so, who is on it and why? What do they do?
  4. Do you have an advisory board? If so, who is on it and why? What do they do?

Financials

  1. How much money do you have to spend on your business?
  2. What are the sources of this funding?
  3. What new sources of funding did you develop during your experience?
  4. How will you spend the money you have during this experience?
  5. How did you spend the money you had during this experience?
  6. Have you already generated any revenues from this idea? If so, how much?
  7. How much money did you generate during this experience?
  8. How was this money generated? Who were your customers?

Exit Strategy

  1. Do you intend to keep this business or do you have an exit strategy?
  2. Do you intend to keep this business or do you have an exit strategy?
